WikiQuiz does not collect, store, or share any personal information. The extension operates locally on your browser with minimal data processing required solely to generate quizzes.

Information We DO NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, email, address)
  • Account credentials or login information
  • Browsing history or tracking data
  • Cookies for advertising or analytics
  • Location data
  • Financial information

Information Processed Locally

The following data is processed and stored locally on your device only:

User Preferences

  • Quiz difficulty setting (easy, medium, hard)
  • Number of questions preference (4 or 7)
  • Sidebar visibility state

This data is stored using Chrome's local storage API and never leaves your device.

Session Data

  • Wikipedia article content (title, sections, summaries) from pages you actively use the extension on
  • This data is stored temporarily in session storage and is automatically cleared when you close your browser

Information Sent to Our Server

When you generate a quiz, the following is sent to our secure proxy server:

  • Wikipedia article content (text from the section you selected for quiz generation)

This content is:

  • Used solely to generate quiz questions via AI models
  • Processed in real-time and not stored permanently on our servers
  • Not associated with any personal identifiers
  • Not used for any purpose other than quiz generation

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect information:

  • All communication with our proxy server uses HTTPS encryption
  • Our server implements rate limiting to prevent abuse
  • No user data is logged or stored permanently on our servers
  • Session data is automatically cleared when you close your browser

Data Retention

  • Local preferences: Stored until you uninstall the extension or clear browser data
  • Session data: Automatically cleared when you close your browser
  • Server-side: No data is retained; content is processed and immediately discarded

Your Rights and Choices

You have full control over your data:

  • Clear local data: Remove all extension data by uninstalling WikiQuiz or clearing your browser's extension data
  • Disable the extension: You can disable WikiQuiz at any time through Chrome's extension settings
  • Modify preferences: Change your quiz settings at any time through the extension popup

Children's Privacy

WikiQuiz does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13 years of age. Since we do not collect personal information from any users, this extends to all age groups.

Permissions Explained

WikiQuiz requests the following Chrome permissions:

Permission Purpose
activeTab Access the current Wikipedia page to extract content for quiz generation
storage Save your preferences (difficulty, question count) locally
tabs Detect when you navigate to Wikipedia pages
alarms Maintain server connection for optimal performance
host_permissions Fetch article content from Wikipedia's API

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
